About me

Beulah Suleman has over 2 years of experience as a project manager in the development sector, specifically focusing on preventative healthcare. Currently, Beulah is a program manager at EDEN Foundation, where she focuses on initiating, designing, and implementing activities on maternal and neonatal child health.

Beulah holds a bachelors in Social Studies from the Obafemi Awolowo University, alongside a masters degree in Social Studies. Beulah is driven by her commitment to pregnant women , new mothers and children. She hopes to witness a society with zero preventable maternal and child healths.

Upon completion of the eHealth Africa Academy programme on Software and Website Development, Beulah plans to use her new skills in designing responsive healthcare systems that help vulnerable pregnant women and newborns have access to lifesaving interventions.
