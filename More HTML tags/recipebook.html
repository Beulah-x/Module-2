Waffle Cookbook

This cookbook contains delicious and crispy recipes of the Belgian and American waffles.

Belgian Waffles

Ingredients Steps
  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • eggs
  • milk
  • vanilla extract
  • butter
  • preheat the waffle maker
  • In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar and baking powder
  • In another bowl, mix egg, vanilla extract, butter and milk
  • Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  • Fill a measuring cup full of the combined batter and pour into the waffle maker
  • Once the waffle is cooked, serve with butter, maple syrup and any fruits of your choice.

American Waffles

Ingredients Steps
  • flour
  • baking powder
  • brown sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • eggs
  • milk
  • melted butter
  • preheat the waffle maker
  • In a large bowl, sift flour, and baking powder with salt
  • In another bowl, whisk together sugar, milk, and eggs
  • Combine both dry and wet ingredients
  • Fill a measuring cup full of batter and pour into the batter
  • Once waffle is ready, serve with a topping of your choice
